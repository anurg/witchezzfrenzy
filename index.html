Their Story

There is a small town amidst the valley of beautiful blue mountains called ‘Casper’. The town has been home to several beautiful women and thrifty wives. Everything seemed perfect and like a paradise in this town for many years. But suddenly once a comet passed over the crystal blue skies of Casper town, call it a myth or superstition, since then the women of the town faced an adversity. An ominous man once passed the stream of this town dropped a poisonous potion into its water. When the people of this town drank the infected water, it caused several kids to choke and die instantly, women saw changes to their appearance from their untouched angelic beauty turning them into a frenzy of witches making their husbands and men leave their wives and women of town to their own fate. This left the women of Casper now turned into witches, wounded but it in no way broke their spirit. They gathered together and strengthened their willpower turning the small town of Casper into a cosmopolitan of opportunities and hope. Now, it’s this frenzy of witches that run the whole town of Casper once runned by men. These witches are anything you can imagine and become. From a pilot to a sailor to a soldier to a cowboy to a captain to a doctor or teacher etc. They are strong-willed witches that are independent and capable of doing anything. They are an enduring spirit of community unaffected by their appearance and people’s judgement, moving forward with the progress of town Casper with an unwavering will that binds them together so called as, “Witchezz Frenzy”.
